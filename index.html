Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Indian former professional cricketer and Captain of India(2007-2017)

"You don't play for the crowd, you play for the country."

-M. S. Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian former professional cricketer, captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats(2007 to 2017) & in Test cricket(2008 to 2014), right handed wicket-keeper batsman. Under his captaincy, India won the ACC Asia Cup two times, in 2010 and 2016. India also won ICC Test Championship Mace two times in 2010, 2011 and ICC ODI Shield for one time in 2013 under his leadership. He is considered as one of the greatest Captains and Wicket Keeper-Batsmen of all time. Throughout his 15 year long international career, Dhoni has won several awards and accolades.

  • Born : 7 July 1981, Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), India
  • Nickname : Mahi, Captain Cool, Thala
  • Profession : Cricket Player
  • Role : Wicket-keeper batsman
  • Known for : One of the best ODI captain the game of cricket, Wicket keeping skills, Leadership skills
  • Spouse : Sakshi Dhoni
